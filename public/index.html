How Orders Are Calculated

The Copy Order button generates suggested orders for any tank at warning level or below 25%. The order amount is calculated by:

  1. Determining available ullage up to the product cap
  2. Filling compartments from largest to smallest without exceeding the cap
  3. Recommending the largest combination of compartments that fits

⚠ Orders are suggestions only. Always verify tank levels and confirm with your fuel vendor before placing an order.
